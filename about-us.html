About EA Media

In an era where communication shapes the world's narrative, EA Media emerges as a brand for innovation, strategy, and transformative storytelling. Our brand identity is not merely a reflection of our services but a testament to our commitment to reshaping the landscape of public relations and business consultancy.

Vision

To become the leading brand for impactful communication, driving growth, and innovation for businesses, leaders across the globe.

Mission

To empower brands and individuals through strategic media engagement, innovative advertising solutions, and business consultancy, fostering a culture of excellence, authenticity, and visionary leadership.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Pioneering cutting-edge strategies that set our clients apart in a crowded marketplace.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of transparency and ethical conduct in all our endeavors.
  • Excellence: Delivering unparalleled quality and precision in every project.
  • Empowerment: Enabling our clients to reach their fullest potential through bespoke solutions and strategic insights.

  • Visionary: We tell stories and share perspectives into the future, anticipating trends and shaping the future of public relations.

Meet the Founder

Emmanuel Agida is a distinguished entrepreneur, business consultant, and PR strategist with a proven track record in media relations and business development. He has a strong niche in crisis management, marketing, public affairs, corporate governance, investor relations, talent management, customer service, and partnership activation.

Emmanuel has served as a media manager for a senior director of Coca-Cola Nigeria and as a media director for a commissioner in the Osun State Government. In addition to his work in public relations, he is passionate about good governance, youth development, and social development.

He is the founder of the Emmanuel Agida Foundation, where he runs several initiatives to empower women, children, and youth. Emmanuel is currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Politics at the University of Benin and a certificate in International Politics from the International Business Management Institute in Berlin, Germany. He is also an honorary doctoral fellow of the Chartered Institute of Public Resources Management and Politics in Ghana.
